Sponsor an Orphan

Sponsor an Orphan from Hogar de amor y protección al niño A.C in Colima, Mexico

Join us in spreading love beyond our borders this holiday season. We’re partnering with an orphanage, Hogar de amor y protección al niño A.C, in Colima, Mexico to bring joy and much-needed support to children in need.

The Home of Love and Protection of Children was born with all the desire to be able to help orphaned, destitute or abused children, who could be given all the support and attention that they would have in a real home, both in material and emotional needs. and spiritual. This is why the mission of Casa Hogar is to provide love and protection to children who, due to various circumstances, lack a home, providing them with food, clothing, housing, education, psycho-pedagogical, medical and social work support, seeking personal fulfillment and development, in a healthy environment according to their age.
