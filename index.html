WELCOME TO RIVO

Rivos Tri-Blend Integrated Curriculum combines the independence of Montessori, the expressive depth of Reggio Emilia, and the joy of play-based learning creating confident, capable, and curious learners.

Why Choose RIVO?

State-of-the-Art Facilities

A meticulously designed environment where every corner inspires curiosity, creativity, and comfort for your child and family.

Joyful Discovery

We cultivate a vibrant atmosphere where learning is an adventure, sparking wonder and delight in every child's daily journey.

Holistic Environment

Seamlessly blending nature-inspired aesthetics with purposeful design to create a serene, grounding space for growth.

Values-Driven Community

Fostering a culture of grace, kindness, and responsibility, we empower children to become compassionate global citizens.

World-Class Educators

Our team consists of passionate, highly-trained professionals dedicated to nurturing your child's potential with warmth and expertise.

Personalized Attention

With exceptional low teacher-to-child ratios, we ensure every child receives the focused care and responsiveness they deserve.

Our mission is to provide high quality care where children learn through play and where parents can work and unwind all under one roof.

Creative School

At RIVO, "Learn and Play" is more than a philosophyit is the heartbeat of our curriculum. Our licensed infant, toddler, and Nursery School programs immerse children in hands-on discovery through STEM, culinary arts, constructive play, and creative expression. We empower young learners to explore, innovate, and grow at their own unique pace, guided by nurturing experts. Here, education is seamless, natural, and as joyful as play itself.

Outdoor Discovery

At RIVO, play is a vital form of learning. Our thoughtfully designed outdoor environment offers a safe, stimulating space where children freely explore, move, and connect. Featuring modern, soft-surface equipment, our playground invites year-round adventure, fostering essential physical development and social interaction in a secure, nurturing setting.

Community

At RIVO, we foster a space where families grow and relationships deepen. From celebrating lifes big milestonesbirthdays, holidays, and achievementsto cherishing simple joys like movie nights, our communal spaces are designed to bring people together. Start with your family, and build a thriving community with us!
