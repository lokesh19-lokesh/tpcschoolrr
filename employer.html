RIVO: A Solution for Your Workforce

Built in Flexibility

  • Our centers operate from 8:30am - 5:00 pm to accommodate various work schedules, with part-time and full-time enrollment options.

  • Our team creates a tailored benefit structure aligned with the budget and culture of companies we work with.

Intentional Pedagogy

  • Research-driven, play based and exploratory curriculum that prepares children for pedagogical excellence, while nurturing social-emotional development.

  • Expert facilitators hold degrees in early childhood education and receive ongoing professional development to stay on the cutting edge of innovative child development practices.

On site or Remote

  • Whether employees are working hybrid or remote, RIVO offers a variety of personal and community working spaces with unique common area, and lounge, kitchen and private phone pods

  • RIVO offers opportunities for parents in similar life stages a chance to create meaningful connections through a variety of parent-focused events, networking opportunities, family seminars and fun activities.

Subsidizing Tuition: How It Works

  • Select a subsidy amount that fits your budget, from partial tuition coverage to full sponsorship for your employees' children.

  • Define which employees qualify based on factors like length of employment, position, or family needs.

  • We handle all the paperwork, making implementation seamless with minimal administrative burden on your HR team.

  • Access our resources to maximize available tax benefits and credits for providing dependent care assistance.
