Schedule a Tour

1. Schedule a Tour

Please fill out this sign up for tour form by clicking the ‘Schedule’ button.

Schedule

2. Submit an application

Please fill out and submit the admissions application and pay the application fee. Click on the ‘Application’ button below.

Application

3. Meet & Greet with Family

Once you have submitted your application, we will contact you to schedule a family meet-and-greet with our facilitators. This is a great opportunity to ask any questions and ensure that our goals align with your family's vision for your child.

4. Finalize Enrollment

To finalize enrollment, please review your admissions offer and complete the following steps:

  • Sign the Tuition Agreement

  • Submit a non-refundable enrollment deposit

  • Finally we will send you information on how to log in to the RIVO Parent Portal to access essential school resources, including a list of what to bring, parenting tips, and more.

Important Details

Enrollment Fees

Once a student is offered admission, a non-refundable deposit is required to secure their spot at RIVO. This deposit will be applied toward enrollment fees

Families enrolling multiple children at our school receive a 10% tuition discount for the second child and any additional children.
